University Research Computing Website

Prior to this site, the Research Computing Group at the University of South Dakota did not have a central location to provide documentation and allow for sign-ups to workshops.

Hosting this site on Github Pages allowd us faster turnover for updates that you would typically not see when relying on a University's Web Development team.

All faculty, staff, students, and partners can access this site for information on upcoming workshops, hardware updates, research showcases, and possible system outages.
